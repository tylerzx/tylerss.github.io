Disney+ is the streaming home of Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, and more. From new releases to your favorite classics and exclusive Originals, there's something for everyone.

Access unlimited entertainment with Disney+ Basic or Disney+ Premium:

  • Disney+ Basic: Disney+ (With Ads) for the price of $7.99/month

  • Disney+ Premium: Disney+ (No Ads) for the price of $10.99/month or $109.99/year

With Disney+, you can choose from an always-growing collection of stories.

  • New releases and timeless classics

  • The entire Skywalker Saga and much of the Marvel Cinematic Universe

  • Beloved and experimental shorts

  • Documentaries, unscripted series, and so much more
